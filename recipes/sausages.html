Sausage Balls

Description

Sausage Balls are made with just 4 simple ingredients sausage, Bisquick, shredded cheddar cheese, and milk. This easy appetizer is the perfect appetizer, party food, or tailgate food!

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Bisquick
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine sausage, biscuit baking mix and cheese. Form into walnut size balls and place on baking sheets.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, until golden brown and sausage is cooked through.
